My Top Tips

  1. Start Speaking Early - Don't wait until you're "ready"
  2. Learn in Context - Study words and phrases in sentences, not isolation
  3. Use Multiple Resources - Don't rely on just one app or method
  4. Practice Daily - Even 15 minutes is better than nothing
  5. Embrace Mistakes - They're part of the learning process
  6. Find Your Motivation - Connect learning to your personal goals
