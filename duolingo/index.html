💡 DET Preparation Tips

  • ✅ Practice consistently for short daily sessions (15–30 min)
  • ✅ Focus on weak areas in grammar and vocabulary
  • ✅ Take mock DET tests regularly
  • ✅ Record and review your speaking tasks
  • ✅ Use English media (news, podcasts, videos) daily
  • ✅ Review mistakes and track your progress
